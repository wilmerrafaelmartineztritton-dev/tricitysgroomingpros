Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a grooming session take?

Typically, a full session takes between 1.5 and 2.5 hours depending on your dog's size, coat condition, and temperament. We never rush to ensure a stress-free experience.

Do you accept aggressive or anxious dogs?

Yes! Our staff is certified in low-stress handling. We recommend mentioning any behavioral notes when booking so we can allocate extra time and care for your pet.

What products do you use?

We use only veterinarian-approved, hypoallergenic, and eco-friendly shampoos and conditioners, tailored to your dog's skin and coat type.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may incur a 50% fee to cover staff time.
